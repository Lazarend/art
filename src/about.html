About

I have been doing art for 15 years.
A family tradition, because grandfather and father are artists.
I create paintings that inspire. Thought-provoking plots and discussions.
A variety of styles and colors provide mood in the interior of a home or office. At the same time, they calm, harmonize and positively affect the mood.
I try to convey complex things in simple terms.
Each picture is written to jazz music, in a good mood, with good people thoughts.
The picture is brought into the house and it should radiate positivity, happiness and warmth is achieved by a simple rule: "If you feel good, create".
